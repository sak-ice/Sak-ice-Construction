Who We Are

Building Nigeria's
Infrastructure Since 2009

Sak-ice Construction Limited is a registered engineering and construction company (CAC RC NO 1603693) committed to delivering exceptional civil, electrical and industrial engineering projects across Nigeria.

Our team comprises highly skilled professional engineers and technicians who have accumulated vast experience from leading contracting and consultancy firms. We bring this expertise to every project we undertake — large or small.

From borehole drilling and water works to large-scale commercial construction, Sak-ice Construction is your trusted partner for reliable, quality-driven engineering solutions.
