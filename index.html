Who We Are

We Create & Turn
Vision into Reality

Sak-ice Construction is involved in contracting and supply jobs in the civil, electrical and industrial engineering services. The company is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) RC NO 1603693 and is made up of highly skilled professional engineers and technicians with vast experience across leading firms in Nigeria.

We are also involved in borehole drilling, water works, pipelines, drainage and other allied engineering works.
